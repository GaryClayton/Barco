    Privacy Policy

    The Bury St Edmunds Abbey Rotary Club (BARC) raises funds for local charities, BARC collects company contact information that is used in conjunction with arranging events.

    In line with this stated aim of raising funds for local charities, BARC collects company contact information that is used in conjunction with arranging its events.

    BARC does not send general advertising emails and any contact is specific to an event we are organising.

    BARC also does not supply any compay or individual specific information to any third party.

    Access to individual contact information is limited to specific members of BARC that are directly involved in organising events and is not availble to the wider BARC membership.

    If you wish to find out what information is held, and/or ask for it to be removed, please use the Contact link provided.
